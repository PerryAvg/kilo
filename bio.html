Meet DJ/ Producer: KILO




University of Miami based producer/DJ, KILO, is quickly taking over his college campus. From opening for Marshmello and D.R.A.M. to racking up thousands of plays on each release, KILO, a.k.a. Perry Avgerinos doesn’t show any signs of stopping his climb to the top. Artists such as Steve James, Big Z, Almand, and others have supported his releases across Soundcloud and live at various shows and festivals nationwide.

KILO plans on releasing a string of new records in 2018. Perry Avgerinos, born and raised outside of Philadelphia, is a current student in Miami Business School with a Minor in Music Business from the Frost School of Music.
